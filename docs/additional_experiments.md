## 2. Mechanism-Probing Experiments

### 2.1 Reciprocity Knockout Study

**Motivation:** Isolate causal role of reciprocity (H6)

**Design:**
- Start with high-K simulation
- Systematically reduce reciprocity:
  - Force asymmetric TE (drain one agent population)
  - Block bidirectional communication

- Measure K degradation slope

**Hypothesis:** ΔK/Δr ≈ 2 (reciprocity has ~2× weight of other harmonies)

**Explanatory Power:** Identifies most critical harmony

**Motivation:** Which harmonies amplify each other?

### 2.3 Temporal Asynchrony Test

**Motivation:** Does synchrony matter for coherence?

**Design:**

**Hypothesis:** K degrades gracefully until variance > 5×

**Explanatory Power:** Tests robustness to heterogeneity
